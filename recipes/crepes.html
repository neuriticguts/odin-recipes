Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Sugar
  1. Mix milk, flour, and an egg and a little sugar uitil you are satisfied by the quantity and consistecy
  2. Medium heat on pan
  3. Lubricate pan with lubricant of choice
  4. Pour liquid(hopefully) mix in the pan
  5. Flip once when the first side is done
  6. When the second side is done remove the crepe from the pan
  7. Sereve with fruit jam(mandatory)
